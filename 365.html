Project Description

I wanted to have a project that keeps me honest dedicated to an idea. 365OfThanks is an idea that I had where I put on display exactly what I was thankful for that day. I wanted to show that even on the worst days, you can always be thankful for something. I started out very strong and through the first 150 days, I thoroughly enjoyed this project, but like every project, you hit a point where you sort of lose love for it. As the project went on, I began to get annoyed due to different factors, for example, no wifi, forgetting to post it on Instagram or getting behind on the website. This project was a great learning experience for me and made me enjoy doing my own projects outside of work and taking things one day at a time.
