Project Description

While working for Old Dominion Freight Line, the business needed a customer facing website to gain business which results in more revenue. Being the lead designer for the projecct, I had to meet with the business to distinguish what the requirements were while meeting with users to validate the UI/UX. One key function of the website is the Instant Estimate which we simplified this process for a decrease of 50% for user clicks.
