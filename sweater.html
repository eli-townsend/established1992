Project Description

In 2014, I was asked to create a shirt that would be handed out at the Old Dominion Frsmall-12 medium-8 Line Christmas concert. For the shirt design, I decided to go with a ugly Christmas sweater theme due to the popularity of that style and to feel out the business. Low and behold, the business got a kick out of the shirt and I still hear murmurs of people asking if we will be getting those back in stock.
